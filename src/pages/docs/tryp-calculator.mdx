## $TRYP Amplifier

### Apostles
It requires **1 [BYOPill](/docs/byopills)** to stake an Apostle in order to generate [$TRYP](/docs/tryp).

Can apply a maximum of 3 BYOPills to increase $TRYP generation.

### BYOLand
You can generate [$TRYP](/docs/tryp) from a [BYOLand](/docs/byoland) by default. A pill is not required for staking.

Can apply a maximum of 1 BYOPill to increase $TRYP generation.
